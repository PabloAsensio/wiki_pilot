## Leadership Styles

![Leadership Styles Lewin](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/a1/Leadership_Styles_Lewin.png)

1.  **Autocratic**: Leader imposes decisions. Good for emergencies (time-critical), bad for morale/team input.
2.  **Democratic (Synergistic)**: Leader consults team. Best for complex problems (using all resources) but slower.
3.  **Laissez-Faire**: Leader is passive ("hands-off"). Can lead to confusion and lack of direction.
    *   **Ideal Pilot**: Situational Leader (shifts between Autocratic and Democratic depending on the phase of flight and urgency).

## Group Dynamics
*   **Synergy**: The output of the team is greater than the sum of the individuals (1+1=3).
*   **Risky Shift**: Groups tend to take riskier decisions than individuals would alone (diffusion of responsibility).
*   **Compliance**: Subordinates agreeing with the leader just to please them (Trans-Cockpit Authority Gradient).
    *   **Steep Gradient**: Captain dominates, FO silent.
    *   **Flat Gradient**: Too casual, lack of command.
    *   **Ideal**: Optimum gradient where authority is respected but questioning is encouraged.
