## Benefits vs. Costs
*   **Benefit**: Reduces physical workload, precise navigation, fuel economy.
*   **Cost**: Increases mental workload (monitoring), risk of skill decay (manual flying skills atrophy).

## Automation Issues
*   **Complacency**: Over-trusting the system. "It's always right." The pilot stops monitoring (out-of-the-loop).
*   **Mode Awareness**: Not knowing what the autopilot is doing.
    *   *Question*: "What is it doing now?" "Why did it do that?"
    *   *Cause*: Silent failures or mode reversions (e.g., reverting from VNAV to Level Change unexpectedly).
*   **Automation Surprise**: When the system behaves differently than the pilot's mental model.

## Golden Rules
1.  **Aviate, Navigate, Communicate**.
2.  Use the appropriate level of automation for the task.
3.  If things don't look right, **disconnect** and fly manually.
4.  Understand your FMA (Flight Mode Annunciator). It is the only reliable source of automation status.
