# Engine Vibration Monitoring (EVM): Accelerometers, Filtering, and Alerts

Gas turbine (jet) engines operate very smoothly. Therefore, any detectable vibration is a critical warning sign of potential mechanical failure, such as rotor imbalance or bearing wear.

## Monitoring System
An **Engine Vibration Monitoring (EVM)** system is installed to detect and quantify these vibrations before damage occurs.
*   **Sensors:** Uses **accelerometers** (often piezo-electric) to measure radial and transverse vibrations.
*   **Signal Processing:** The raw signal is **filtered** to remove normal engine operational frequencies/noise, isolating only those frequencies indicative of malfunction.
*   **Indication:** The filtered signal is amplified and displayed in the cockpit. The display typically shows a **unit-less value** (relative scale) rather than a specific physical unit.
