## Integrated Aeronautical Information Package
Aeronautical information is published as an integrated package that includes:
1.  **AIP** (Aeronautical Information Publication).
2.  **AIP Supplements**.
3.  **NOTAM** and Pre-flight Information Bulletins (**PIB**).
4.  **AIC** (Aeronautical Information Circulars).
5.  Checklists and lists of valid NOTAM.

## Post-flight Information
For international aerodromes, arrangements must be made to receive information from aircrews regarding:
*   State and operation of navigation facilities.
*   **Presence of wildlife hazards**.
This information is channeled to the AIS for distribution as necessary.

## Information on the Movement Area
Must be reported without delay:
*   Construction work.
*   Rough surfaces.
*   Snow, ice, water.
*   Failure of visual aids / power supply.
*   Daily inspections are required (at least once for codes 1-2, twice for 3-4).
