This European Commission regulation establishes common requirements for providers of air traffic management (ATM) and air navigation services (ANS).

## Scope
*   Covers the provision of ATM/ANS for general air traffic.
*   Includes meteorological services (Part-MET), AIS services, and flow management.
*   Establishes oversight and certification of such providers by competent authorities.

*Note: The regulation emphasizes safety, quality, and management of human and technical resources in the provision of navigation services.*
