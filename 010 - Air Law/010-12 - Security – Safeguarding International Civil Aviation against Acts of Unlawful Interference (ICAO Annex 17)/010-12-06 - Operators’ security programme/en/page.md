# Operators' Security Programme: Annex 17 Requirements for Commercial and General Aviation

## Operator's Programme
*   Each Contracting State shall ensure that commercial air transport operators establish, implement and maintain a written **operator security programme**.
*   This programme must meet the requirements of the National Civil Aviation Security Programme.

## General Aviation
It is recommended that general aviation operations using aircraft with a maximum take-off mass greater than **5,700 kg** also establish and maintain a written security programme.
