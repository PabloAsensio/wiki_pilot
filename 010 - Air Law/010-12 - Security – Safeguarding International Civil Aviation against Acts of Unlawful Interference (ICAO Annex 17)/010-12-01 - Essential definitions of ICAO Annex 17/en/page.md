## Key Security Concepts
Annex 17 deals with the protection of civil aviation against **acts of unlawful interference**.

*   **Security Control:** Means by which the introduction of weapons, explosives, or other dangerous devices into civil aviation is prevented.
*   **Screening:** The application of technical or other means which are intended to identify and/or detect weapons, explosives or other dangerous devices.
*   **Aircraft Security Check:** An inspection of the interior of an aircraft to which passengers may have had access and an inspection of the hold for the purposes of discovering suspicious objects.

> The primary objective is the safety of passengers, crew, ground personnel, and the general public.
