## Mobile Objects
*   **Emergency vehicles:** Preferably **Red** or **Yellowish Green**.
*   **Service vehicles:** Preferably **Yellow**.
*   All mobile objects must be marked with colors or flags.

## Fixed Objects
*   **Lighting:**
    *   Low, medium, or high intensity obstacle lights.
    *   Must be visible from all angles in azimuth.
*   **Marking (Flags):**
    *   Flags to mark fixed objects must be **Orange** or a combination of **Orange and White** (or Red and White).

## Overhead Wires
*   **Marker Balls:**
    *   Must be displayed alternately in **White and Red** or **White and Orange**.
    *   Minimum diameter: 60 cm.
    *   Purpose: To make visible wires (high tension lines, river crossings) that pose a hazard to navigation.
