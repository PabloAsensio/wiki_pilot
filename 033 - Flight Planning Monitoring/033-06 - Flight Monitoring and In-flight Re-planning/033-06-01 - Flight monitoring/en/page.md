# Flight Monitoring


## Navigation Monitoring
*   **Track Check**: Confirming aircraft position relative to planned track every 15-30 mins.
*   **ETA Updates**: Revising Estimated Time of Arrival at next waypoints based on actual Ground Speed.
*   **Gross Error Check**: Verifying next waypoint distance and track before passing current waypoint.

## Fuel Monitoring
*   **Fuel Checks**: Comparing *Actual Fuel on Board* vs *Planned Fuel* at each waypoint.
*   **Discrepancy**:
    *   If burning more than planned: Determine cause (wind, higher level, engine issue).
    *   Project future consumption to ensure destination reserves are intact.
*   **Top of Climb Check**: Critical check to verify climb performance and initial cruise fuel flow.

## System Monitoring
*   **Engine Instruments**: Oil pressure/temp, EGT, N1/N2 within limits.
*   **Pressurization**: Cabin altitude climbing/descending correctly.
*   **Electrical/Hydraulic**: Loads and pressures normal.
