## Rudder
The primary control surface for yaw (rotation around the vertical/normal axis).
*   **Action**: Rudder Left -> Tail moves Right -> Nose Yaws Left.
*   **Secondary Effect**: Yaw leads to Roll (due to speed difference on outer wing and dihedral effect).

## Rudder Limiter
At high speeds, full rudder deflection could cause structural failure of the vertical fin due to excessive aerodynamic loads.
*   **System**: A limiter restricts the maximum rudder deflection as airspeed (CAS) increases.
*   **Variable Stop**: The physical stop moves inward as speed increases.

## Uses of Rudder
1.  **Coordinate Turns**: Oppose adverse yaw.
2.  **Crosswind Landing**: Align the longitudinal axis with the runway (de-crab) or maintain sideslip (wing down).
3.  **Engine Failure**: Check yaw towards the dead engine.
