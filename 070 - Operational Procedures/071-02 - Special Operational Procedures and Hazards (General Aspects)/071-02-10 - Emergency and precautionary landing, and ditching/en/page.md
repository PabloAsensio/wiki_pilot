## Types
*   **Forced Landing**: Immediate landing due to inability to continue flight (e.g., dual engine failure, fire).
*   **Precautionary Landing**: Planned landing due to abnormal situation (e.g., fuel shortage, sick passenger, minor mechanical issue) where continuation is inadvisable but not impossible.
*   **Ditching**: Emergency landing on water.

## Ditching Procedures
*   **Preparation**: Notify ATC (Position, Souls, fuel). Prepare cabin (Life vests ON, loose items secured).
*   **Direction**:
    *   **High Wind**: Land into wind (reduce ground/water speed).
    *   **High Swell**: Land parallel to swell (along the crest) to avoid crashing into wave face.
*   **Touchdown**: Minimum speed, nose high (so tail touches first). Do not stall high.
*   **Evacuation**: Use slide-rafts. Do not use exits that are submerged.

## Brace Position
Reduces secondary impact injuries (flailing).
*   Feet flat on floor.
*   Head down against seat in front (or on knees).
*   Hands protecting head.
