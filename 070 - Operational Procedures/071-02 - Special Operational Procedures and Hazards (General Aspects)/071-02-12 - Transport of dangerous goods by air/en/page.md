## Regulations
*   **ICAO Annex 18**: The Safe Transport of Dangerous Goods by Air.
*   **IATA DGR**: Dangerous Goods Regulations (Operational manual used by airlines).

## Classification (9 Classes)
1.  **Explosives** (Dynamite).
2.  **Gases** (Flammable, Non-flammable, Toxic).
3.  **Flammable Liquids** (Petrol, Alcohol).
4.  **Flammable Solids**.
5.  **Oxidizing Substances**.
6.  **Toxic & Infectious Substances** (Poisons, Virus cultures).
7.  **Radioactive Material**.
8.  **Corrosives** (Acids, Batteries).
9.  **Miscellaneous** (Dry ice, Lithium batteries, Magnets).

## NOTOC (Notification to Captain)
The pilot must be informed in writing about DG on board:
*   Proper Shipping Name.
*   UN Number.
*   Class/Division.
*   Weight/Quantity.
*   Location (Hold number).
*   Emergency Response (Drill Code).

## Forbidden Items
Some items are strictly forbidden (e.g., Strike-anywhere matches, liquid oxygen devices).
